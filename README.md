# Subject Score Calculator

A sleek, intuitive web application designed to help students track their academic progress, calculate weighted assignment results, and determine exactly what marks they need to pass or achieve their target grades.

---

## 🎯 The Pain Point

As a student, keeping track of final grades can be stressful and tedious. 
* **Manual Overhead:** Relying on spreadsheets or pen-and-paper to manually calculate weighted percentages for every assignment.
* **The "What Do I Need?" Anxiety:** Constantly wondering, *"What is the minimum score I need on the final exam to pass this course?"*
* **Pre-Results Stress:** Anxiety building up prior to grade releases due to a lack of clarity on current academic standing.

## 💡 The Solution

The **Subject Score Calculator** eliminates the guesswork and eases pre-exam pressure. By providing a dynamic, real-time dashboard, users can input their assignment weights and scores to instantly see their current running total and visualize their required scores for upcoming assessments. 

### Key Features
* **Dynamic Weighting:** Easily add multiple assessments and assign custom percentage weights.
* **"What-If" Analysis:** Input hypothetical scores for future exams to see how they impact your final grade.
* **Target Grade Predictor:** Instantly calculates the exact score needed on remaining tasks to pass (or hit distinction targets).
* **Responsive Design:** Check your grades on the go via desktop, tablet, or mobile.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend Framework:** ReactJS (Functional components, hooks for state management)
* **Build Tool:** ViteJS (For lightning-fast development and optimized production builds)
